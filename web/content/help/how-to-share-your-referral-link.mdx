# How to share your referral link

<Callout type="success">
WhatsApp is usually the fastest channel for getting qualified signups.
</Callout>

<Image src="/help/referrals-flow.svg" alt="Referral flow from sharing to active status" caption="Share link → invite joins → Active referral → credits earned." />

## Recommended sharing flow

<Steps>
1. Open your referrals dashboard and copy your unique link.
2. Share a short message that explains the benefit clearly.
3. Follow up with your invite after signup.
4. Help them complete their first verified paid event.
</Steps>

## Good message template

- “I use PropatyHub for listings and lead flow. Join with my link and start faster: [your link]”
