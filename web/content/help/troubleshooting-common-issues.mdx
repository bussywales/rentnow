# Troubleshooting common issues

## Quick diagnostics

- Refresh the page and retry once.
- Confirm role and workspace access.
- Check if required fields are complete.
- Capture exact error message and timestamp.

<Callout type="warning">
Do not share passwords or one-time codes with anyone, including support staff.
</Callout>

## What to include in support tickets

- Page URL and action performed
- Time of error
- Screenshot or short screen recording
- Relevant listing/referral/request IDs
