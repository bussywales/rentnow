## What changed
- Added a premium glass-style mobile bottom dock with quick actions for Home, Explore V2, Saved, and Profile.
- Added scroll-aware collapse and expand behavior to keep browsing space while maintaining fast access.
- Added an expandable dock search overlay with instant actions (`Near me`, `Reset`) and direct routing into property search.

## Notes
- The dock is hidden on admin/auth surfaces to avoid interfering with management flows.
- This is a UX refactor only; no setting keys, APIs, or analytics behavior were changed.
