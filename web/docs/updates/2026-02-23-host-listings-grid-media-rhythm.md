## What changed

- The `/host/listings?view=all` listing cards now use a stable `4:3` media ratio.
- Portrait and mixed-orientation photos are cropped consistently with `object-cover` instead of stretching card rhythm.
- Card wrappers now enforce full-width constraints to avoid accidental horizontal spill.

- Host/Agent:
  - Listing management cards feel more uniform and easier to scan.
