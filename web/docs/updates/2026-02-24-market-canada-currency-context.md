## What changed

- Added **Canada (CA$)** as a selectable market option in the market selector.
- Updated market defaults admin options to include:
  - Country: `CA` (Canada)
  - Currency: `CAD` (CA$)
- Clarified market messaging:
  - Market affects **currency display context** only.
  - Market does **not** limit where users can search.

## Why this matters

- International users can select Canada without feeling excluded.
- Market copy now clearly communicates that destination/inventory access remains global.

## Where it appears

- Global market selector in header and mobile drawer.
- Shortlets pricing context helper copy.
