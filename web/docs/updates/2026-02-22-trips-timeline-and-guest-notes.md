## What changed

- Trip detail (`/trips/[id]`) now shows a clear timeline with status progression and a **What happens next** helper.
- Request-mode trips now show explicit host-response expectation (12-hour SLA) in the timeline context.
- A new coordination panel on trip detail lets tenants send a structured note to the host (topic + message) without introducing full chat.
- Host bookings drawer now includes a **Guest notes** section with newest-first notes per booking.

## Why this matters

- Tenants get clearer post-booking confidence and fewer “what now?” moments.
- Hosts receive important coordination context early, improving decision speed and check-in readiness.
- Support receives fewer repetitive status and coordination tickets.
