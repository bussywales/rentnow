## What changed

- Added pilot-specific operator runbooks, validation scorecards, and a stakeholder decision memo template for Move & Ready Services.
- Added minimum host/admin help coverage so the wedge is documented and reachable without tribal knowledge.
- Tightened host/admin copy so the feature reads as a limited-capacity pilot instead of an open-ended services expansion.

## Why it matters

- The wedge can now be run as a controlled pilot with explicit stop/go/iterate rules.
- Operators have clearer daily and weekly review guidance.
- Hosts and admins can reach the feature and its limitations more easily.

## Explicit exclusions

- No tenant requester expansion
- No removals
- No scheduling, payments, payouts, or reviews
- No public provider directory
- No provider subscriptions or lead billing
