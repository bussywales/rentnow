## What changed
- Explore V2 cards now render real listing hero images instead of preview-only placeholders.
- Hero media keeps a persistent premium placeholder behind the image and fades in once loaded to avoid flashes.
- Added lightweight next-card hero predecode on unconstrained networks for smoother scrolling.

## Notes
- This update is image/UI only for `/explore-v2`; Explore V1 and feed composition behavior are unchanged.
