## What changed

- Explore slide overlay now shows explicit pricing copy:
  - Shortlets: `From X/night`
  - Rent: `X/month` (or yearly when listing cadence is yearly)
  - Buy: `X`
- Explore details sheet now uses the same clear pricing language.
- Estimated shortlet total appears only when valid check-in/check-out dates and guests are present.

## Why this helps

- Removes ambiguous pricing formats.
- Keeps pricing easy to parse at a glance.
- Avoids misleading totals when stay inputs are missing.
