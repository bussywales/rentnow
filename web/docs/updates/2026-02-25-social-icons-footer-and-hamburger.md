## What changed

- Replaced footer social monogram pills (`IG`, `YT`, `TT`, `FB`, `WA`) with recognisable platform icons.

## Why it matters

- Social links are faster to scan and more recognizable.
- Footer and hamburger now use a consistent social presentation.
