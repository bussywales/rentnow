## What changed

- Hosts can now configure a dedicated **Guest arrival & house rules** section in shortlet settings.
- Added fields for check-in window, checkout time, access method/hint, parking, Wi-Fi, quiet hours, and policy flags (pets/smoking/parties), plus freeform house rules.
- Trips now shows a structured **Check-in details** and **House rules** panel with server-side redaction by booking stage:
  - not paid: hidden
  - paid + awaiting host approval: limited
  - paid + confirmed/completed: full

## Why this matters

- Guests get clearer, calmer post-booking guidance with less uncertainty.
- Hosts keep sensitive arrival details protected until booking confirmation.
- Support teams get fewer “what happens next” questions after payment.
