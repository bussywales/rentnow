## What changed

- Added stable recommendation reason codes to the local recommendation engine.
- Added one-line reason labels on each **Recommended next** card.
- Added a lightweight **Why?** disclosure in the rail header:
  - clarifies recommendations are based on saved, viewed, and last browse signals
  - confirms signals are stored locally on this device

## Why this helps

- Makes recommendations easier to trust and understand.
- Keeps recommendations transparent without adding backend tracking.
- Preserves non-biased, market-scoped, local-first behaviour.
