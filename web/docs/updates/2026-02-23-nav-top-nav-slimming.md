## What changed

- Desktop top navigation now keeps only critical role-based links:
  - Tenant: `Shortlets`, `Properties`, `Trips`, `Saved`
  - Host/Agent: `Bookings`, `Calendar`, `Listings`, `Earnings`
  - Admin: `Admin`
- `Notifications` and `Profile` remain directly accessible in the top bar.
- Lower-priority links are now intended to be accessed via the hamburger menu.

## Why it matters

- The header now stays focused on high-frequency actions and reduces decision friction.
