## What changed

- Added a one-time `Tap for details` hint in Explore. Once dismissed or used, it stays hidden on that device.
- Added local-only `Not interested` via long-press on the slide image to hide the listing from Explore.
- Added a 5-second Undo toast after hiding a listing.
- Added a `Similar homes` mini rail (up to 3 items) inside the details sheet.
- Improved details CTA trust microcopy:
  - Shortlets: quick checkout reassurance
  - Rent/Buy: no-commitment viewing reassurance

## Why this helps

- Reduces first-run friction in Explore.
- Increases listing-to-detail and detail-to-next-listing loops without adding clutter.
- Keeps controls transparent, local-first, and non-biased.
