## What changed

- Updated `/host/earnings` summary cards to group totals by currency instead of combining different currencies into one amount.
- Single-currency accounts still render the same one-line total display.
- Multi-currency accounts now show separate lines per currency and a hint: “Totals shown by currency.”

## Why it matters

- Hosts and agents no longer see misleading combined totals when bookings and payouts span multiple currencies.
- The page remains conversion-safe without introducing FX assumptions or exchange-rate drift.
