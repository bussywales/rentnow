## What changed

- Adjusted the `Market-aware picks for homes` cards on `/properties` to use a consistent fixed-height shell.
- Kept the tag and trust badges in the upper portion of each card.
- Moved the title, description, and `Explore` call-to-action into a bottom-aligned content block so the cards visually line up.

## Why it matters

- Mixed title and subtitle lengths no longer make the discovery cards feel uneven.
- The rail reads more like a curated set instead of cards with drifting content baselines.
- The primary action stays in a predictable place across all featured picks.
