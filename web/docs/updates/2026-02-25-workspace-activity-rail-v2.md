## What changed

- Upgraded the `/home` activity rail with typed badge chips for key events: lead received, booking request, listing approved, payout requested, payout paid, and support escalation.
- Added clearer per-item action links (for example: open lead, open booking, open listing, open earnings, open support).
- Enriched feed items with explicit metadata and severity so the rail can stay consistent as event sources grow.

## Why it matters

- The workspace feed is easier to scan and act on quickly.
- Hosts and agents can move directly from each activity item to the right surface without guessing where to click.
