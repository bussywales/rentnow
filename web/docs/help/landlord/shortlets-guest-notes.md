## What changed

- Hosts can now read **Guest notes** inside the booking details drawer on `/host/bookings`.
- Notes are tied to a booking and sorted newest first.
- Tenant notes are also sent through in-app notifications and email to reduce missed context.

## Where to find guest notes

1. Open `/host/bookings`.
2. Select a booking row and click **View**.
3. In the drawer, scroll to **Guest notes**.

If there are no notes yet, the section shows **No guest notes yet**.

## How to use notes operationally

- Prioritize urgent requests first, then read guest notes before approval.
- Use notes to confirm arrival constraints and check-in readiness.
- Keep blocks and availability updated if the note changes expected timing.
