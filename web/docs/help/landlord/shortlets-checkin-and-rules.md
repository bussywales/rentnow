## Where to configure

1. Open `/host/shortlets/[id]/settings`.
2. Find **Guest arrival & house rules**.
3. Save details such as:
   - check-in instructions
   - check-in window and checkout time
   - access method and access hint
   - parking, Wi-Fi, and house rules
   - quiet hours, pets, smoking, and parties policy

## What guests can see

- Guests only see these details once payment has succeeded.
- If a request booking is awaiting approval, guests see a limited version:
  - house rules and check-in window
  - no access-sensitive hints
- Full details appear after confirmation.

## Good practice

- Keep arrival instructions short and practical.
- Avoid posting sensitive full codes in plain text.
- Use the preview card to verify clarity before saving.
