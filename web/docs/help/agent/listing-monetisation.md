## When to use this guide

Use this guide when your portfolio hits the free posting threshold or a listing is blocked until billing is resolved.

## The current agent billing path

PropatyHub checks whether your workspace has enough listing coverage before a listing can move forward.

That coverage can come from:

- remaining free posting capacity,
- listing credits already attached to your workspace,
- an active subscription plan,
- a one-time pay-per-listing payment when that option is enabled.

## What happens when the free posting threshold is reached

After the free posting threshold is used, new publish, renewal, or reactivation steps can trigger a billing gate.

The next step depends on what your workspace still has available:

- available credits -> the listing can continue,
- no credits + pay-per-listing enabled -> you can pay once for that listing,
- no credits + pay-per-listing unavailable -> you must choose a plan.

## When subscriptions apply

Choose a plan when you want ongoing listing capacity instead of paying for listings one by one.

Plans are the better fit when you:

- publish regularly,
- manage higher listing volume,
- want predictable monthly coverage,
- want included listing and featured credits.

## When a one-time listing payment applies

A one-time listing payment is only for the listing currently being blocked.

That is useful when:

- you need one listing live now,
- you are not ready to move onto a monthly plan,
- the paywall specifically offers a per-listing payment option.

It does not replace an active plan for future listing volume.

## Where to manage it

Open [`/dashboard/billing#plans`](/dashboard/billing#plans) to:

- compare plans,
- see current listing and featured credits,
- continue to the billing flow when a listing is blocked,
- confirm whether your workspace is still on Free or an active paid plan.

## Important notes

- Listing billing and featured billing are separate.
- If a listing is blocked by billing, saving the draft does not clear that requirement.
- If your plan has expired or been removed, the workspace falls back to current available coverage only.
