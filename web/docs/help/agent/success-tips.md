## Weekly growth routine

1. Refresh top listings media and descriptions.
2. Review demand signals from saved searches and home rails.
3. Promote high-converting listings with featured requests.
4. Audit response times and viewing follow-through.

## Raise conversion quality

- Keep inventory clean and truthful.
- Use trust signals in your follow-up conversations.
- Focus on response quality, not only speed.

## Team discipline

- Keep SOPs in help docs current.
- Log edge cases for admins with reproducible detail.
- Publish concise product updates for host/agent audience after each launch.
