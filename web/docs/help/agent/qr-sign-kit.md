## What it does

The QR sign kit creates a tracked offline-sharing asset for one live listing in your managed portfolio.

Each kit includes:

- a tracked listing link,
- a QR code built from that link,
- a larger sign-style asset,
- a smaller QR card asset.

## Who can use it

Agents can use QR sign kits on live listings they manage.

## When it appears

The QR sign kit is available only for live listings. If the listing is not currently live, the share panel does not let you generate the kit.

## How to use it

1. Open the live listing.
2. Open the listing share panel.
3. Choose **Open QR sign kit**.
4. Switch between **Sign kit**, **QR card**, and **QR only**.
5. Download the selected asset or copy the tracked link.

## What happens after someone scans it

- If the listing is still live, the scan opens the live listing through the tracked PropatyHub share path.
- If the listing is no longer live, the QR does not bypass state and the old listing content is not shown as if it were still available.

## What the outputs are for

- **Sign kit**: better for on-site or printed display.
- **QR card**: better for smaller handouts or compact print space.
- **QR only**: best when you already have your own layout and only need the code.
