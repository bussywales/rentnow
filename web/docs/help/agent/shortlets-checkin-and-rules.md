## Agent workflow

- Confirm the listing has complete **Guest arrival & house rules** fields in host shortlet settings.
- Validate check-in window, checkout time, and clear house rules before publish.
- Encourage hosts to keep access hints concise and non-sensitive.

## Guest visibility rules to communicate

- Before payment success: check-in details are hidden.
- Paid but awaiting approval: guest sees limited rules and timing context only.
- Confirmed stay: guest receives full arrival details.

## Why this matters

- Better clarity reduces support tickets and no-show risk.
- Controlled visibility keeps operational details safer before confirmation.
