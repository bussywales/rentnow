## When this appears in Trips

- Open `/trips/[bookingId]` and look for **Check-in details** and **House rules**.
- Visibility depends on payment and booking status.

## What you will see by status

- `Payment not succeeded`: details are hidden with guidance to complete payment first.
- `Paid + awaiting host approval`:
  - You see limited details (house rules and check-in window).
  - Access-sensitive hints are hidden until approval.
- `Paid + confirmed/completed`: full details are shown (arrival instructions, access method, parking, Wi-Fi, and rules when available).

## Why some info is hidden at first

- PropatyHub protects hosts and guests by showing sensitive arrival details only after booking confirmation.
- This reduces entry-risk while still giving you enough context to plan your stay.
