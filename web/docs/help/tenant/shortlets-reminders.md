## What reminders you receive

For paid shortlet bookings, PropatyHub may send:

- 48 hours before check-in
- 24 hours before check-in
- 3 hours before check-in
- Morning of checkout

Reminders are delivered in-app and by email when available.

## Why reminders matter

- Helps you prepare arrival details in advance.
- Reduces missed check-in and checkout timing issues.
- Keeps trip status clear without needing chat threads.

## Check-in details from host

- Hosts can share check-in details directly from the bookings inbox.
- When shared, you get a trip notification and email with arrival guidance.
